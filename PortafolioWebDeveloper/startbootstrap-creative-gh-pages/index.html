About

Welcome to my Portfolio website. My name is Rafael Suárez and I am a Web and Mobile developer based in Toronto, Canada. I have a Baccalaureate in Language Teaching and I also have more than 14 years experience in the Airlines industry, as a Cabin Crew member at Avianca Airlines in Colombia and at Westjet Canada. I have always been interested in educating myself especially about new technologies. If you are seeking to reinforce your team with an enthusiastic, efficient and creative Web developer, my portfolio will be of interest. You will find a brief of web and mobile development experience and skills required for a fast-paced team environment.
